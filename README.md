
# Veterans India Dataset Analysis – Talent Insights & Workforce Profiling 

Veterans India is a national, apolitical organization focused on promoting patriotism, social responsibility, and nation-building through youth engagement, awareness programs, and community-driven initiatives.

This project analyzes internship applicant data for Veterans India to uncover trends in skills, education, and location preferences, enabling data-driven decision-making for program planning and candidate allocation.

## Overview  
This project explores internship applicant data collected for Veterans India programs.

A structured workflow was followed:

- Extract and structure raw dataset  
- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing  
- Conduct exploratory data analysis (EDA)  
- Identify trends in skills, domains, and preferred locations  
- Generate insights for data-driven decision-making  

---

## Business Problem  
Veterans India requires insights into internship applicants to efficiently allocate candidates and design impactful programs.

Key objectives:

- Identify high-demand skills among applicants  
- Understand geographic and domain distribution  
- Optimize candidate-role matching  
- Improve outreach and engagement strategies

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)  

- Candidate distribution across Technical vs Professional domains  
- Skill analysis (Python, SQL, Web Development, AI/ML)  
- Geographic distribution (State-wise & City-wise)  
- Education stream and specialization trends  
- Preferred job location insights  

---

## Research Questions & Key Findings  

### 1. Candidate Distribution  
- Total Candidates: **500+**  
- Technical Domain: **~70%**  
- Professional Domain: **~30%**  

### 2. Skills & Demand Trends  
- Most common skills: **Python, SQL, Web Development, Data Analytics**  
- Strong presence of **AI/ML and Full Stack Development profiles**  
- Increasing trend in **Data Analytics and Business roles**  

### 3. Geographic Insights  
- Top preferred cities: **Bangalore, Hyderabad, Delhi NCR, Pune**  
- Candidates from **20+ states across India**  
- High concentration from **Karnataka, Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, Telangana**  

### 4. Education & Specialization  
- Majority from **B.Tech, BCA, MCA backgrounds**  
- Key domains:
  - Data Analytics  
  - Web Development  
  - AI/ML  
  - Finance & HR  

---

## Final Recommendations  

- **Skill-Based Matching:** Align candidates with roles based on Python, SQL, and analytics skillsets  
- **Location Optimization:** Focus opportunities in high-demand cities like Bangalore & Hyderabad  
- **Targeted Outreach:** Engage high-participation states for better conversion  
- **Program Design:** Introduce role-based internship tracks (Data, Tech, Business)  
- **Dashboard Monitoring:** Use Power BI/Tableau for real-time insights
